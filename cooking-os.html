Pack Format Guide

A valid Cooking Pack must include: dish, description, emoji, ingredients, and steps array.

Each ingredient is an object with: name, quantity, essential (true/false), and optional note. Simple strings also work as a fallback.

Each step needs: title, instruction, duration_min, duration_max, heat, and optional sensory_cue, failure_risk, stir_interval, skill_tags, uses_ingredients, prep_notes (array of strings — shown as a live checklist during cooking). Top-level pack_version tracks your pack revisions.
